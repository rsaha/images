New Delhi - India's Capital and Heritage site

New Delhi is not only the administrative capital of India. Delhi is also a city that actually bridges two different cities together. The old Delhi was once a part of the Islamic reigns. It is a city with narrow roads and formidable mosques. There are also several historical monuments and dilapidated havelis. On the other hand, the comparatively new city of New Delhi was established by the British rulers. It is a city of tree lined avenues, spacious government buildings and structures. New Delhi is also known for its rich cultural background. Explore the city and soak in the gems that lie all around like the ancient monuments and the fascinating galleries, the wonderful architecture, and the bustling markets.
